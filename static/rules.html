What is Chengyu

Cheng Yu are traditional Chinese idiomatic expressions that consists of four Chinese characters.

Chengyu Wordle

Our 成语了 is very similar to wordle. Your task is to guess the chengyu in 5 turns according to the description writen above the puzzle. There is a line to remind you how many turns you have left below the puzzle. Each guess needs to be a valid chengyu.

What the Colours Mean

  • Yellow -- The character is in the chengyu but not in the correct position
  • Green -- The character is in the chengyu and in the correct position
  • Grey -- The character is not in the chengyu
